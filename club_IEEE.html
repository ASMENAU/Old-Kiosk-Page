Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers
Type of Organization: Professional development and student projects based organization.
Organization Description:
IEEE is an electrical engineering club here at the Engineering building that provides a place to make friends, learn key concepts through projects and presentations, hone skills and develop a feeling of pride for being a student in Engineering. We really hope that you come out and get involved in the Institute! Please remember to bookmark our webpage as it will be your guide to all NAU-IEEE events, services, the executive elections and much more! Feel free to give us a visit at our office during the regular school year in Engineering Building, room 237.
