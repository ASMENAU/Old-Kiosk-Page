Camp Verde Bridge Design
Team Members: Dylan Brancato, Anna VanMeter, Jeremy Beeson & Alex Powers
Abstract:
The Town of Camp Verde Arizona has experienced significant flooding on Verde Lakes Dr. from spring runoff and summer monsoons, causing road closures 15 to 20 days a year. The Northern AZ Engineering Inc. student capstone design team in collaboration with the Camp Verde Public Works Director, Ron Long, has created a 30% Bridge design intended to be used as an informative source to mitigate the flooding along the road. This design includes general bridge concepts applicable and material cost estimates applicable to Verde Lakes Dr.
