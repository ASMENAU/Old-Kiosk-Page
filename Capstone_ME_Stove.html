Stove Team
Team Members: Matthew Acker, Matthew Verstraete, Adam Ellis & Scott Calkins
Abstract:
The STOVE capstone team consists of 4 senior Mechanical Engineering students at Northern Arizona University. Stoves objective is to improve the JetBoil camping stove�s efficiency by boiling 16.1 liters of water per 100grams of fuel. That will be a 14% increase from the current JetBoil.
Design Objective:
The camp stove will be able to bring 13.6 liters of water from 70°F to boiling temperature, using 100g of fuel. This is a 14% improvement on the efficiency of the most fuel efficient JetboilTM stove on the market (JetboilTM is the stove provided to the team by the sponsor), and a 5% improvement on most efficient stove currently on the market, the ETA PackLite.
