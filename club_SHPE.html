Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers
Type of Organization: Professional development and service based organization.
Organization Description:
The Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers @ NAU focuses on the development of its members not only academically but also professionally. We send our members to conferences in which they develop leadership and networking skills all you need to attend is the motivation and drive to be a success! We also engage in outreach programs, fundraisers, and other fun activities geared towards the overall success of the membership. We also provide our members with beneficial workshops and guest speakers during our meetings. So if you are interested in having fun, life lasting friends, giving back to the community, and that push to become a successful engineer/leader join the SHPE NAU family!
