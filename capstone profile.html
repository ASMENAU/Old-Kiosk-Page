Abstract:
The NAU Aero12 project is designed for the Aero West competition which will take place from March 16th,2012 to March 18th, 2012 at Van Nuys in California. The competition has been designed to provide exposure to the kinds of situations that engineers face in the real work environment. The airplane must be designed and built by the SAE student members without direct involvement from professional engineers, radio control model experts, pilots, machinists, or related professionals.
Design Objective:
The objective of Regular Class is to design an aircraft that can lift as much weight as possible while observing the available power and aircraft's length, width, and height requirements. Accurately predicting the lifting capacity of the aircraft is an important part of the exercise, as prediction bonus points often determine the difference in placement between competing teams.
