American Society of Mechanical Engineers
Type of Organization: Professional development and student projects based organization.
Organization Description:
Our student chapter of the American Society of Mechanical Engineers focuses on providing mechanical engineering students a great experience by providing the ability for professional development, project based design work and fun social activities. The club works on multiple projects such as the Human Powered Vehicle, Student Powered Design Competition and this Kiosk. The club also provides the opportunity to listen to guest speakers and take tours of interesting engineering sites. Lastly the club also focuses on providing professional development opportunities through events with the professional chapter of the organization and an annual Resume and Interview Workshop.
