Design of a Covered Patio for Hewes Family Residence
Team Members: Katie Hein, Ann Taylor & Joseph Blumberg
Abstract:
The Hewes family of Flagstaff, Arizona requested a design of a patio cover to eliminate snow accumulation along the rear entrance of the residence and over the existing patio slab. The current situation makes the patio inaccessible throughout the winter months. JAK Engineering was to evaluate the existing structure and to develop a patio design that would be appropriate for the location and incorporate the aesthetic ideas of the client. Once the patio design was finalized with the client's approval, the team prepared all necessary documentation to be presented to the city as a new structure. With the city's approval of the design, the patio cover is planned to be constructed, Summer 2012.
Details:
The Design team will prepare the Covered Patio Structural Design including all plan sheets and calculations. The structural plans, prepared on CAD, will include a site plan with index, a foundation plan with foundation cross section details, a typical cross section, a framing plan with framing cross section details, a roof/truss layout plan, all architectural treatments, and an elevations plan.
