American Indian Science and Engineering Society
Type of Organization: Professional development and service based organization.
Organization Description:
Promote excellence, leadership, and opportunities in education and professional development for American Indians and Alaska Natives. We enrich the fields of science, engineering, and other academic areas with Native traditions and to strengthen the communities with knowledge, resources and innovation.
