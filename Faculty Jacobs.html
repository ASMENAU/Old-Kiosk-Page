Professor Steven Jacobs

Biography:

Steven M. Jacobs retired from 32 years in the aerospace industry, holding various technical and executive management positions. He is now Adjunct Faculty of Computer Science at Northern Arizona University, Flagstaff, Arizona.

He was formerly with Northrop Grumman Mission Systems in Carson, California. Mr. Jacobs managed engineers developing user interface and web applications software for various government and commercial applications. Professor Jacobs was Director of Software at Beckman Instruments (now Beckman-Coulter) in Brea, CA, a medical equipment company serving customers worldwide. Mr. Jacobs was also Adjunct Assistant Professor at the University of Southern California for 17 years, where he developed and taught their graduate computer science courses in user interface design and human performance engineering.
