Shareview
Team Members: Waylon Dixon, Bryce Carey, Britt Mullen & Chad Ellsworth
Abstract:
The mass connectivity enabled by the Internet and mobile devices has revolutionized the kinds and amounts of information that can be shared between individuals. By leveraging this connectivity, the Shareview project will build the infrastructure and user interface to enable social networking that centers on the sharing of video streams from one user to another: The system will support the creation, transfer, and sharing of video streams between users, the persistent storage of video streams, and a rich interface that will allow users to customize what streams they see and which of their own streams the public has access to.
