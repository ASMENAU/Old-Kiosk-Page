Urban Concept Vehicle
Team Members:
Abstract:
The Urban Concept Vehicle team at Northern Arizona University consists of five sub-groups: Chassis and Suspension, Interior & Fairing, Drive train and Motor control, Steering and Braking system, and Electrical Engineering team. Together, the Urban Concept Vehicle team will work to build a fuel efficient vehicle which will represent the skills and knowledge of the students from engineering department. This Vehicle will also be used as a showcase at NAU and participate for Shell-Eco Marathon competition which is held annually in Houston, Texas.
